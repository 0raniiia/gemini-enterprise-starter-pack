# UC-MKT-04 — Structured Creative Brief Generation

**Function:** Marketing | **Tool:** Gemini | **Difficulty:** ⭐ Beginner | **Time saved:** ~80%

---

## The Problem

A poorly written creative brief is expensive: endless back-and-forth with agencies, misaligned deliverables, missed deadlines. Yet writing a solid brief takes 1–2 hours — and most teams don't even have a standard template.

## The Solution

Paste your raw notes (meeting notes, client email, product data) and Gemini structures a professional, agency-ready creative brief in minutes.

---

## Prompt Template

```
You are a senior marketing director. I need to write a creative brief for [PROJECT_TYPE, e.g. "a LinkedIn awareness campaign"] and I need you to structure it professionally.

Here is my raw input:
[PASTE YOUR NOTES HERE — email, product info, bullet points, anything you have]

Generate a complete creative brief with the following sections:

1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVES
   - Market context (3 lines)
   - Primary objective (SMART format if possible)
   - Success KPIs

2. TARGET AUDIENCE
   - Primary persona (demographics, psychographics, digital behavior)
   - Key consumer/buyer insight

3. KEY MESSAGES
   - Core promise (1 sentence)
   - Supporting proof points (max 3)
   - Communication tone

4. DELIVERABLES
   - List of formats with technical specs
   - Legal / compliance constraints

5. TIMELINE & BUDGET
   - Approval phases
   - Key milestones

6. REFERENCE MATERIALS
   - Inspiring campaign examples to search
   - What to absolutely avoid

Output format: professional document, structured, ready to send to an external agency.
```

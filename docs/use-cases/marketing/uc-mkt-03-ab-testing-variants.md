# UC-MKT-03 — A/B Testing Variant Generation

**Function:** Marketing | **Tool:** Gemini | **Difficulty:** ⭐ Beginner | **Time saved:** ~85%

---

## The Problem

Campaigns perform better when you test multiple variants. But generating 5 different email subject lines, 3 ad headlines with distinct angles, or 4 alternative CTAs takes disproportionate time — and teams often lack inspiration to go beyond their usual style.

## The Solution

Gemini generates structured variants by psychological angle (urgency, direct benefit, curiosity, social proof, FOMO) in seconds. Test more. Optimize faster. Lower your CPL.

---

## Prompt Template

```
You are an expert B2B copywriter specializing in conversion optimization.

Context:
- Company: [COMPANY_NAME]
- Offer: [OFFER_DESCRIPTION, e.g. "HR management software for SMBs"]
- Target: [PERSONA, e.g. "HR Directors at companies with 50–500 employees"]
- Campaign goal: [GOAL, e.g. "registration for a free webinar"]
- Constraint: [CONSTRAINT, e.g. "email subject max 50 characters, no clickbait"]

Generate:

A) 5 EMAIL SUBJECT LINES with a different angle each:
   - Angle 1: Direct, quantified benefit
   - Angle 2: Provocative question / curiosity
   - Angle 3: Urgency and scarcity
   - Angle 4: Social proof / benchmark
   - Angle 5: Pain point recognition

B) 4 LANDING PAGE HEADLINES (H1, max 10 words each) using the same angle variety

C) 3 CTA BUTTON TEXTS (3–5 words): one rational, one emotional, one focused on immediate benefit

For each variant, add a short note explaining the psychological principle behind the choice.
```
